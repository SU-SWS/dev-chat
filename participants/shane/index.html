Lexical Enrichment Tool

A scholarly utility maintained by the Department of English for the refinement of faculty correspondence, lecture notes, and manuscript prose. Submit a passage to receive an enriched rendering calibrated for rhetorical depth and lexical precision.

Enriched rendering

A note on usage. The Lexical Enrichment Tool is provided as a courtesy to Stanford faculty and affiliated scholars. Output is generated by algorithmic substitution and should be reviewed prior to publication or distribution.
